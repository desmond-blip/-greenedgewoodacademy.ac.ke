Library Usage Guide

The Green Edgewood Academy Library is open to all pupils and staff for academic growth and reading enrichment. Pupils can read, borrow, and return storybooks, revision books, and learning materials.

How to Borrow a Book:

  • Ask the librarian or use the catalog to find your book.
  • Present your library card to borrow.
  • Each pupil may borrow up to 2 books for 1 week.

How to Return a Book:

  • Return books on or before the due date.
  • Late returns will attract a small penalty.

Library Rules and Regulations:

  • Maintain silence and cleanliness.
  • No food or drinks allowed.
  • Handle all books with care.
  • Respect library staff and other readers.
