Green Edgewood Academy

Mr. Desmond Elliot

As the Deputy Head Teacher of Green Edgewood Academy, I take this opportunity to address all teachers, learners, and support staff on the importance of discipline and teamwork in building a successful school. Discipline forms the cornerstone of learning, respect, and responsibility. Together, we can maintain an environment that nurtures excellence and good character.

To the learners: your future success begins with how you conduct yourselves today. Respect your teachers, follow school rules, and give your best effort in every activity.

To the teachers: let us continue setting positive examples and fostering a caring yet disciplined atmosphere for our learners.

To our non-teaching staff: your contribution in maintaining order and supporting school operations is deeply appreciated. Let’s all work together toward a better, brighter Green Edgewood Academy.

🌟 Vision

To nurture confident, disciplined, and academically excellent learners who positively impact society.

🎯 Mission

To provide holistic, quality education in a safe and inclusive environment that promotes integrity, creativity, and lifelong learning.
